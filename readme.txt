== Description ==
 
 My-Bookings-RES gives you the ability to link your my-bookings.org account to your wordpress site.
 Once you have linked your my-bookings.org account with your wordpress site you can start synchronizing your apartments or hotel rooms to your wordpress site.
 After that guests can easily view and book your apartments or hotel rooms on your wordpress site. 

 
== Installation ==
 
1. Visit 'Plugins > Add New'
2. Search for 'My-Bookings-RES'
3. Activate 'My-Bookings-RES' in your installed plugins overview.
4. Now visit the plugin's page via the newly created menu entry to the left and follow the setup instructions.
